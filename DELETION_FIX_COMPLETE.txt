ISSUE RESOLVED
================================================================================

Problem: After deleting an employee, the deleted employee still appeared in 
the employees section after refreshing the page.

Root Cause: The backend GET /api/employees endpoint was returning all 
employees (including terminated ones) when no status filter was provided.

Solution: Modified the backend to exclude terminated employees by default 
for admin/HR users, while still allowing them to view terminated employees 
if they explicitly filter by status.
