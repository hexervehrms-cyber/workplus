# Asset Photo Upload - User Guide

## Quick Start

### For Admin/HR Users

#### Adding Photos When Creating an Asset
1. Go to **Admin Dashboard** → **Assets**
2. Click **"Add Asset"** button
3. Fill in asset details (Name, Type, Category, Model, Serial Number, etc.)
4. Scroll down to **"Asset Photos"** section
5. Click the upload area or drag-and-drop image files
6. (Optional) Add descriptions for each photo
7. Remove any unwanted photos using the X button
8. Click **"Add Asset"** - photos will upload automatically

#### Managing Photos After Asset Creation
1. Find the asset in the grid
2. Click the **"Photos"** button on the asset card
3. In the gallery modal:
   - Use arrow buttons to navigate between photos
   - Click thumbnails at the bottom for quick navigation
   - Click **"Set as Main"** to make a photo the thumbnail
   - Click the trash icon to delete a photo
   - Click **"Add More Photos"** to upload additional photos

### For Employee Users

#### Viewing Asset Photos
1. Go to **Employee Dashboard** → **My Assets**
2. Find the asset you want to view
3. Click the **"Photos"** button on the asset card
4. In the gallery modal:
   - Use arrow buttons to navigate between photos
   - Click thumbnails at the bottom for quick navigation
   - View photo descriptions and upload dates
   - Click **"Close"** to return to asset list

## Features

### Photo Upload
- **Drag & Drop**: Drag image files directly onto the upload area
- **Click to Browse**: Click the upload area to select files from your computer
- **Multiple Files**: Upload up to 10 photos at once
- **Descriptions**: Add optional descriptions to each photo
- **Preview**: See photo previews before uploading

### Photo Gallery
- **Carousel Navigation**: Use arrow buttons to browse photos
- **Thumbnail Grid**: Click thumbnails for quick navigation
- **Photo Counter**: See which photo you're viewing (e.g., "Photo 1 of 5")
- **Photo Info**: View description and upload date
- **Main Photo**: Star icon indicates the main/thumbnail photo

### Photo Management (Admin/HR Only)
- **Set as Main**: Make any photo the asset's thumbnail
- **Delete**: Remove unwanted photos
- **Add More**: Upload additional photos without closing the gallery

## Tips & Best Practices

### Photo Quality
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Avoid blurry or dark images
- Include multiple angles (front, back, sides)
- Show serial numbers and identifying marks

### File Formats
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- Recommended: JPEG for photos, PNG for screenshots
- File size: Keep under 5MB per image for best performance

### Organization
- Upload photos in logical order (front, back, sides, details)
- Set the clearest photo as main/thumbnail
- Add descriptions for special features or damage

### Descriptions
- Be specific: "Front view with serial number visible"
- Note any damage: "Scratch on bottom left corner"
- Include context: "Assigned to John Doe on 2026-05-03"

## Troubleshooting

### Photos Won't Upload
**Problem**: Upload button is disabled or doesn't work
- **Solution**: Ensure you have admin or HR role
- **Solution**: Check that asset was created successfully
- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page and uploading again

### Photos Not Showing
**Problem**: Photos uploaded but don't appear in gallery
- **Solution**: Refresh the page
- **Solution**: Close and reopen the gallery
- **Solution**: Check browser console for errors

### Can't Delete Photos
**Problem**: Delete button is grayed out or doesn't work
- **Solution**: Ensure you have admin or HR role
- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page
- **Solution**: Check that you're not trying to delete the only photo

### Gallery Won't Open
**Problem**: Photos button doesn't open gallery
- **Solution**: Ensure asset has photos uploaded
- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection

## FAQ

**Q: Can I upload photos after creating an asset?**
A: Yes! Click the "Photos" button on any asset card and use "Add More Photos" to upload additional photos.

**Q: Can employees upload photos?**
A: No, only admin and HR users can upload, delete, or manage photos. Employees can only view photos.

**Q: What happens if I delete a photo?**
A: The photo is permanently removed from the asset. If it was the main photo, another photo is automatically set as main.

**Q: Can I change the order of photos?**
A: Photos are displayed in upload order. You can set any photo as main to make it the thumbnail.

**Q: How many photos can I upload?**
A: You can upload up to 10 photos at a time. You can upload more photos later by clicking "Add More Photos".

**Q: What file formats are supported?**
A: JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats are supported.

**Q: Can I add descriptions to photos?**
A: Yes! When uploading photos, you can add optional descriptions for each photo.

**Q: Where are photos stored?**
A: Photos are stored in the database as part of the asset record.

**Q: Can I download photos?**
A: Currently, photos can only be viewed in the gallery. Download functionality may be added in future updates.
