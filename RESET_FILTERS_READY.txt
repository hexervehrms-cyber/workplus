FILTER BAR LAYOUT:

[Filter Icon] [Category ▼] [Status ▼] [From Date] [To Date] [Apply Filters] [Reset Filters]

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HOW IT WORKS:

1. User applies filters (Category, Status, Date Range)
2. List shows filtered expenses
3. User clicks "Reset Filters"
4. ✅ All filters cleared
5. ✅ Category → "All Categories"
6. ✅ Status → "All Status"
7. ✅ From Date → (empty)
8. ✅ To Date → (empty)
9. ✅ List shows all expenses
10. ✅ Toast shows "Filters reset"
